Red Dialer Call Tracker - Easy Mobile Guide (v1.1 - Offline Mode)

This tool helps you contact lawmakers. Your progress is saved automatically to this device.

1. First Time Opening? (Quick Setup):

  • A "Welcome" box will pop up.
  • Type in your Full Name and City/Town.
  • Tap "Save and Continue." This info is saved to your device.
  • Need to change it later? Tap "Edit My Info."

2. Sending Emails:

  • Email templates are provided. You can edit them on the main screen.
  • Changes to templates are saved automatically.

3. Contacting Lawmakers:

  • To Call: Tap the phone number. After, check "Called." A box appears to log the response and notes. Tap "Save Response."
  • To Email: Tap the email address. Your email app opens. After sending, the "Emailed" box is checked automatically.
  • All these actions are saved automatically.

4. Your Progress:

  • Auto-Save: All changes (checkboxes, notes, templates, user info) are saved automatically to your device. The "Save All Changes" button has been removed.
  • Export/Import: Still available for manual backups or transferring to a *new* device.
  • Reset All Data: Clears data from *this device's local storage*. Use with caution!
